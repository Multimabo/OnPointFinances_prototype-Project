About OnPointFinances

Enabling financial freedom in our community since 2015.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, OnPointFinances began with a simple mission: to provide accessible financial solutions to the Zwelethemba community. What started as a small office in the Thusong Center has grown into a trusted financial services provider, helping hundreds of clients achieve their financial goals.

We understand the unique challenges faced by our community members and have tailored our services to meet these needs with affordable loans, investment opportunities, and funeral cover that provide real value.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To empower our clients with financial solutions that are accessible, transparent, and designed to improve their quality of life.

Vision

To be the most trusted financial services provider in our community, known for our integrity and client-focused approach.

Values

Integrity, Transparency, Community Focus, Empowerment, and Excellent Service.
